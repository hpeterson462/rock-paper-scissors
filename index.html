When you play the game of thrones, you win or you die

The rules in this game are very simple. Two champions are chosen and whoever picked the winner lives. The odds look even at first, but luckily you know a thing or two more than Jon Snow.

    Each of the three possible champions are guaranteed to win over another:
  • Rock will always beat Scissors
  • Scissors will always beat Paper
  • Paper will always beat Rock

Choose your champion and start the trail if you dare..
